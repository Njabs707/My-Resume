Responsibilities

Technical Work:

  • Executed production machine/equipment setups and changeovers for diverse job requirements.
  • Optimized production machine/equipment settings to enhance output efficiency.
  • Conducted troubleshooting and basic repairs on production machinery during and after operational hours
  • Performed corrective maintenance tasks on malfunctioning machines to minimize downtime.
  • Implemented procedures for setting up machines to improve cycle time and reduce downtime.
  • Conducted monthly preventive maintenance on production machines to ensure smooth operations.
  • Expertly disassembled and reassembled production machinery as per procedure/manufacturer specifications for maintenance purposes.

Administrative Work:

  • Efficiently booked jobs into the SAP system before commencing production.
  • Completed all essential documentation on schedule while adhering to GMP (Good Manufacturing Practice) guidelines.
  • Maintained close communication with the production manager to validate priorities and report production readiness.
  • Regularly liaised with supervisors to ensure adherence to the production schedule.

Quality Assurance and Data Analysis:

  • Conducted quality checks on products throughout the production process
  • Collected accurate run-time data to identify improvement opportunities, presenting findings to senior management.

Proactive Problem Solving:

  • Demonstrated foresight by anticipating potential future issues such as low component stocks (leaflets/cartons) and technical faults, implementing preemptive measures to save time.

Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) and Compliance:

  • Adhered strictly to Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) to maintain process consistency and quality standards.
  • Actively participated in deviation management and change control activities to ensure regulatory compliance.

Environment, Health, and Safety (EHS) Involvement:

  • Served as the representative for Environment, Health, and Safety (EHS) matters within the department, fostering a safe and compliant work environment.
